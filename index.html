About the project

The Pew Charitable Trusts’ Voting Information Project and The Internet Association, in conjunction with the world’s leading technology companies, launched “Get to the Polls” to help voters find all the information they need before voting November 4, 2014.

Get to the Polls, powered by the Google Civic Information API, allows voters to find their official polling place, hours of operation, and full ballot summary based on their residential address.
